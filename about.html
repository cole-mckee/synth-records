Who We Are

Our Story

Welcome to Synth Records, where the rhythm of innovation meets the soul of sound.

At Synth Records, we believe in the transformative power of music. Since our inception, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting an unparalleled platform for artists who dare to push boundaries, explore new sounds, and connect with audiences on a profound level.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to amplify unique voices in the electronic and synthwave music scenes while preserving the rich heritage of these genres. From dreamy retro beats to cutting-edge electronic anthems, we work hand-in-hand with our artists to bring their visions to life and ensure their music resonates far and wide.
